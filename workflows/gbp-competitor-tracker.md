# GBP Competitor Tracker Workflow

## Goal

Compare a selected GBP listing against local competitors.

## Inputs

- selected business
- target location or service area
- competitor list or discovery query
- public competitor signals

## Outputs

- competitor summary
- review count and velocity comparison
- posting activity comparison
- category and service gaps
- media activity gaps
- visible offer or event opportunities

## Safety

This workflow is read-only and report-focused.
