# GBP Optimizer Workflow

## Goal

Audit a selected Google Business Profile listing and generate optimization recommendations.

## Inputs

- selected business config
- selected GBP listing
- listing details
- services and categories
- hours and attributes
- reviews
- photos
- posts
- Q&A

## Outputs

- audit summary
- missing keyword opportunities
- weak SEO signals
- missing images and content suggestions
- suggested services or categories
- suggested FAQs
- suggested post ideas
- approval-ready recommendations for any live changes

## Safety

This workflow is read-only by default.
