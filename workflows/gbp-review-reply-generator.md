# GBP Review Reply Generator Workflow

## Goal

Generate review reply drafts and extract review intelligence.

## Inputs

- selected GBP listing
- review text and rating
- business tone settings
- target keywords
- prohibited wording

## Outputs

- reply draft
- sentiment summary
- praise themes
- complaint themes
- service quality trends
- suggested profile or post improvements

## Safety

Replies are drafts until approved.
