# GBP Competitor Tracker

## Workflow

1. Load selected business config.
2. Load manually supplied competitors or discover competitors through future integration.
3. Compare review counts, review velocity, categories, services, posts, media, offers, and visible events.
4. Identify content gaps and media opportunities.
5. Produce a human-readable competitor report.

## Safety

This skill is read-only and report-focused.
